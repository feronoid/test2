You Will Be Provided With A PSN Account Having An Active Subscription / Game License As Per Your Requirement, You Are Required To Login Into Your Console, Activate Primary And Download Games Simple

Primary Access Allows User To Share License Of Purchased Producs To All The Existing Users Of The Console , So You Will Be Able To Play Games / Online Multiplayer In Your Own Account

STEPS FOR GAMES INSTALLATION:

1. Go To The "Settings>Login Settings>User Managment".

2. "Create" A New User & Login With The Email:Pass That I Provide You.

3. Activate Your PS4 As Primary From:
Settings >PlayStation network/Account Management>Active as Your Primary PS4

4. Go To "Library>Purchased" & Add The Games To Start Downloading.

5. Now Restore Licence From:
Settings> Playstation Network/Account Managment> Restore licence

6. Log-out & Log-in With Your Main Account, Now You Can See Games Downloading In Your List.

7. Do Not Remove The Account From Your Users.
